Welcome to yum yum food! This site is dedicated to all things yummy food. If you're new here, please check out the about section. When you've been acclimated to the site, take a look at and browse through the recipes section for all of our tasty dishes.


about


Here at yum yum food, we take pride in bringing you the freshest and the best recipes for all of your cooking and baking needs. We love food, and we want you to love your food, too!


us


yum yum food was created by Lindsay Jamison and Wendy Fodstad. They met at Butler University in 2007 and have been creating tasty food together ever since. This site was created in 2010 and has exploded with yummy recipes.


photography

We take all of our own photos. Our most recent photos were taken with a Canon Rebel t3i (with the default lens). Please do not use any of the photos without our permission. If you have any questions, feel free to send them our way!
